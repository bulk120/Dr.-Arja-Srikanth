Dr. Arja Srikanth, IRTS — Public Servant & Administrator

IRTS (Retd. 1986) · Special Commissioner, A.P. Bhavan, New Delhi · Expertise in transport economics, PSU management, cultural tourism.
About

Profile

Senior civil servant with decades of leadership across Indian Railways, Central and State government ministries, and PSUs. Experience includes Executive Director roles at NTPC, Private Secretary to Union Ministers (Steel, Coal, Mines), Director Traffic—Ministry of Railways, and senior administrative posts in Andhra Pradesh. Doctorate in Marketing of Cultural Tourism and gold medalist of IRTS (Railway Staff College, 1989).
